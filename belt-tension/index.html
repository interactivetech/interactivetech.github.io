Formula used

T = 4 W L² f² / g

T = belt tension in lbf, W = belt weight per length in lb/ft, L = free span in ft, f = frequency in Hz, g = 32.174 ft/s².

To get W from a whole belt: W = (belt weight oz / 16) / (belt length inches / 12).
